Parents

In the cradle of Ulpara village, within the Vangoor Mandal of Nagarkurnool district, my father's journey thrived. Nurtured by Narasimha Chary and Sugunamma, he embodies the rustic warmth and enduring values of Ulpara. Rooted in the fertile soil of Vangoor Mandal, his life tells a tale of resilience, love, and profound impact.

Studies

My father, guided by his unique journey, chose a different path after completing his studies up to the 12th class. Recognizing that his passion lay beyond the academic realm, he made a thoughtful decision to pursue a profession that resonated with his heart and family heritage. Inspired by my grandfather's legacy as a skilled goldsmith, my dad embarked on a journey to follow in those precious footsteps.

Dad : My Hero
